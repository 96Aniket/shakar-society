Overview of Cooperative Society



Vision of the Department

A vibrant cooperative movement for growth of a sound and sustainable economy.


Mandate of the Department

“To facilitate voluntary formation, democratic functioning and professional management of the cooperative societies as people’s institutions based on self help & mutual and enable them to promote their socio-economic betterment”.


Features of a Cooperative Society

1. Voluntary and open Membership

2. Democratic Member Control

3. Member Economic Participation
4. Autonomy and Independence
5. Education, Training and Information
6. Co-operation among Cooperatives Societies
7. Concern for Community
8. Professional Management
